    Plunder

    I created this game for PTBO Game Jam 04. I worked in a team of 4 people and we had 32 hours to complete a pirate themed game. My teammates and I created a 2D game in the same vein as Galaga. I was in charge of the creating everything for the AI components, score, player life count, and getting assets for the game. I also helped another group member create the player controller. You can view a clip of the game here.

    Undead Defense

    Undead defense is a game I helped make in a group of 5 people. It is a mini game that was initially intended to be put into a class project called "The Spot", where multiple teams made different games and then merged them all into one arcade style game. Although the class project ended up being scrapped due to merge conflicts from certain teams, "Undead Defense" was still completed and submitted. It is a 3rd person tower defense game where the player fends off skeleton zombies while also building towers to help defend the castle. My role was to create a sound manager, AI Movement and damage, as well as getting assets for the game. You can view a clip of the game here.

    Pie Hop

    Pie Hop is the first complete game I created in the Unity Engine. I co-created this with a friend and we went through various changes in the coding process and asset selection. I was in charge of making the Mini Map as well as doing enemy AI, Menus, Time, Score, and getting and implementing assets.The player must try to collect as many escaping fruits as he can before the timer runs out so that he may make pie, while also avoiding enemy bears coming to steal his freshly made pies. You can view a clip of the game here.

    Dragon Soar

    Dragon soar was one of my first attempts at making a video game and was made using Android Studio. I had 5 days to make this game and a limited knowledge of programming to use, but I tried to make do with the resources I could find on the web. I gathered the assets from various websites and figured out how to do most of the programming with help from forums and video tutorials. You can view a clip of the game here.
